About Me


Software engineer with a passion for the Arts. I enjoy drawing, jamming on guitar, and nature walks. To gain access to a new field of employment, I enrolled in a course offered by Tech Louisville in late 2019. After five months of dedication, I achieved a certificate from Google as an Information Technology Support Professional. Upon completion, I became employed by Compucom as an IT Service Desk Support Agent. My responsibilities were receiving incoming calls, and troubleshooting end-user application issues, I also performed account management and maintenance for applications, such as Microsoft Office, VPN, Citrix, and MDM systems. While employed at Compucom, AMPED provided the next opportunity for growth. Accepted as a Data Analyst Immersive Fellow by General Assembly, I created projects using Python, Tableau, PowerBI, and other applications used by Data Analyst.   Through the XCA, a contemporary technology curriculum that teaches students how to code, I became a full-stack engineer working for Humana. Puzzle-solving, creativity, and ingenuity are all elements of software engineering that I love. It is exciting to code. I have found my niche. I have a strong desire to become part of a diverse team and use my fresh skill set to build purposeful next-level software. I am dedicated to the field of Computer Science, developing as an engineer, continued learning, and growth.
