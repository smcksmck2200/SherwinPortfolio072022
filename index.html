About Me

Louisville, Kentucky

Sherwin M. Felton

Software Engineer

Software engineer with a passion for the Arts. I am a graduate of Highland Springs High School. I attended Reynolds Community College to persue my interest in Music Recording Technology, learning the fundementals of this disipline fascinated me. I enjoy drawing, playing guitar, biking and nature walks. In 2019, I was presented with the opportunity to take a course offered by Tech Louisville. My goal was to complete the course to gain access to a new field of employment, the field of Computer Science. I completed the course in five months receiving a certificate from Google as an Information Technology Support Professional.

Soon after completion, I became gainfully employed by Compucom as an IT Service Desk Support Agent. I received incoming calls, diagnosed and troubleshot end-user desktop application issues, and provided an appropriate solution. I also supported end-users in their use of applications such as Microsoft Office, VPN, Citrix, and MDM. I performed account management and maintenance for various applications and systems (e.g., creating or modifying user accounts and permissions, and performing password resets).

While employed at Compucom, I was presented with an opportunity to learn more new skills. Through AMPED I was accepted as a Data Analyst Immersive Fellow. Here I was introduced to Python, Tableau, PowerBI, and other applications used in data analysis. I presented my Capstone project, using data, to show how the police presence in certain neighborhoods directly correlates to increased incarceration rates in those same neighborhoods.

Desiring more knowledge, I learned of XC Academy. XC Academy is a contemporary, human-centered technology curriculum that teaches students how to code. I applied and was graciously accepted as an intern working for Humana. Here, I have become a Full-Stack engineer. I found my niche. I enjoy the problem-solving as well as the creative aspect of software engineering. I am excited to become a part of a team where I get to use my skill set in building production software. I am dedicated to this field, continued learning, and growth. I am confident I can become an asset to any organization.
